  <behavior>
    Expected behaviors in testable terms:

    **S/R Stop Derivation (RISK-02):**
    - Long entry at 65000 with supports [63000, 61000, 58000] -> stop at 63000 (nearest support below entry)
    - Long entry at 65000, proposed stop 64000, supports [63000] -> stop at 63000 (S/R is wider/safer, overrides proposed)
    - Long entry at 65000, proposed stop 62000, supports [63000] -> stop at 63000 (S/R is tighter, still use S/R for structure)
    - Short entry at 65000 with resistances [67000, 70000] -> stop at 67000 (nearest resistance above entry)
    - Entry with empty S/R levels -> uses proposed stop, stop_type="percentage"

    **S/R Target Derivation (RISK-03):**
    - Long entry at 65000, stop at 63000 (risk=2000), resistances [66000, 68000, 72000]:
      - 66000 gives R:R of 0.5 (skip, below 3:1)
      - 68000 gives R:R of 1.5 (skip, below 3:1)
      - 72000 gives R:R of 3.5 (TP1, meets 3:1)
    - Short entry at 65000, stop at 67000 (risk=2000), supports [64000, 62000, 58000]:
      - TP1 = 58000 (R:R = 3.5, meets 3:1)

    **2% Risk Cap (RISK-05):**
    - Account 100000, entry 65000, stop 63000: risk_per_unit=2000, position_size_units = (100000*0.02)/2000 = 1.0
    - Account 50000, entry 65000, stop 63000: position_size_units = (50000*0.02)/2000 = 0.5
    - Without account_size: position_sizing is None, position_size_units is None

    **3:1 R:R Enforcement (RISK-03):**
    - R:R of 2.5 -> law_2_rr="fail", approved=False, rejection_reasons includes R:R violation
    - R:R of 3.0 -> law_2_rr="pass" (exactly meets minimum)
    - R:R of 5.0 -> law_2_rr="pass"

    **Max Position Enforcement:**
    - open_position_count=4, MAX_POSITIONS=5 -> law_3_positions="pass"
    - open_position_count=5, MAX_POSITIONS=5 -> law_3_positions="fail", rejected

    **Dual Mode:**
    - With account_size: position_sizing populated, position_size_units calculated
    - Without account_size: position_sizing is None, position_size_units is None, law_1 always "pass"
  </behavior>
